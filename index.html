Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite.

On a rainy day in 2021, Oluwabukunmi stepped out with a simple prayer in her heart: “God, let me meet a fine man today.” 😊

Little did she know, that prayer was about to be answered sooner than expected. At Suraju Bus Stop, Ikola, a car horn caught her attention and there he was. Everything she had asked for, and more.

What started as a simple moment grew into something truly special filled with love, laughter, and a deep connection.

Today, Oluwabukunmi and Ifeanyi celebrate a love that began with faith, a little boldness, and perfect timing.
